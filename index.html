My name is Meaghan Johnson and I help companies
develop comprehensive strategies and innovative products.

Currently based in Berlin, I am a world traveller and
believer in a borderless world.

I represent the voice of the customer, inform you of industry or market standard, and suggest cultural nuances based on my banking and travel experiences to ensure you can innovate according to your business needs.

Principles

My consulting and design thinking is based on three principles:

1.

Innovation comes from a customer jobs to be done framework.
2.

History and culture are vital ingredients for product development.
3.

Innovators must know their markets, competitors and peers.
